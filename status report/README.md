# Fresh Beats Music Streaming: Strategic BI Revenue & Engagement Report

## 📊 Project Overview
Fresh Beats is a dual-sided music platform that provides streaming for enthusiasts and promotional services for emerging artists. This project involved a comprehensive Business Intelligence audit to evaluate artist promotion effectiveness, optimize user conversion from free to paid tiers, and refine genre-based recommendation strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Challenges
* **Artist Promotion Effectiveness:** Evaluate how promotional campaigns translate into listener exposure and ROI for artists.
* **User Conversion & Retention:** Identify the behavioral drivers that convert "Free" listeners into "Paid" subscribers to ensure sustainable revenue.
* **Content Recommendation:** Tailor genre-based promotions to match shifting user preferences and emerging music trends.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Strategic Recommendations

### 1. User Tier & Engagement Analysis
* **The Insight:** Data revealed that **Paid users have significantly steadier growth and higher retention rates** than Free users. Paid users are also more active, consuming a higher volume of tracks per session.
* **The Strategy:** Implement targeted promotions focused on high-engagement genres to bridge the "conversion gap."

### 2. Genre Trends & Lifecycle
* **The Insight:** **Pop music peaked in 2021** and has since shown a downward trend in average users. Conversely, **Rock remains the most popular genre** for the Free user base.
* **The Strategy:** Focus marketing campaigns on Rock fans with "Free Trial" incentives to convert them to the Paid tier, where retention is historically stronger.

### 3. Emerging Artist Growth
* **The Insight:** Analysis identified **Electronic and Hip Hop** as high-potential growth areas where user interest is rising but the current catalog is under-indexed.
* **The Strategy:** Increase the catalog of Electronic and Hip Hop tracks by offering time-limited "Free Promotion" deals to appropriate emerging artists in these genres.

* **Business Intelligence Reporting:** Translating data visualizations into actionable executive summaries.

* **Trend Interpretation:** Identifying genre lifecycles (Growth, Peak, and Decline) to optimize content acquisition.
